# Datasets used in the analysis

## Sentinel-2 (https://browser.dataspace.copernicus.eu/)

Datasets for the area were downloaded on May 2026 for two sets of dates, 03/02/2026, before the rain season started, which is out draught sample and 04/21/2026 where a couple of considerable precipitation had already fallen, making our 'flood' sample.

Only bands 3 (green) and 11 (SWIR) were used for the calculation of MNDWI

## Indiana DEM Tile Footprints 

Downloaded from Indianamap.org and used for determining the slope and making sure that the area is indeed a wetland
